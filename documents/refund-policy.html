Refund Policy for Alternative Scans

Refund Policy

Last updated: November 01, 2025

At Alternative Scans, we strive to ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with their purchases. We understand that situations may arise where a refund or return may be necessary. Please read our refund policy carefully to understand your rights and obligations regarding refunds, returns, and cancellations.

General Refund Terms

Refunds will be processed only after the returned goods or eligible items have been verified and approved by our team. All refunds will be issued to the original payment method used at the time of purchase. Once the refund is approved, it may take 5–10 business days for the amount to reflect in your account, depending on your payment provider. We reserve the right to reject a refund request if the product does not meet the return conditions mentioned below.

Digital Goods (eBooks, Software, Downloads, etc.)

For digital goods, such as eBooks, software, or downloadable content: Refund requests must be made within 12 hours of purchase. Refunds will only be granted if the digital product has not been copied, downloaded, shared, or distributed in any form. Once a digital product is accessed, downloaded, or otherwise used, it is considered consumed and not eligible for a refund. We rely on system records to determine if a download or access attempt has been made.

Memberships, Subscriptions, or Online Plans

For membership or subscription-based services: Refunds are available within 7 days from the date of purchase or renewal. After 7 days, no refunds will be issued. If you cancel within the 7-day window, you will receive a full refund, provided you have not used or excessively accessed the premium features during that period. Partial refunds are not provided for unused portions of monthly or annual subscriptions once the refund window has expired.

Non-Refundable Items and Exceptions

We do not offer refunds for the following:

●View only books.

●Services that have already been rendered.

●Gift cards, coupons, or promotional credits.

●Downloaded, accessed, or shared digital products.

Policy Changes

Alternative Scans reserves the right to modify or update this Refund Policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes will be reflected on this page with the updated date. We encourage customers to review this policy periodically.
